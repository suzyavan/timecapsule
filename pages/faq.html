Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Virtual Time Capsule

What is this website about?

Our platform lets you create digital time capsules. You can securely upload photos, set a timer, and lock them away until the chosen time has passed. No peeking allowed!

How do I create a time capsule?

First, sign up for an account. Once you're in, you can upload your photos and set a timer—days, months, or even years. Once the timer starts, the capsule is sealed until time runs out.

Can I open or view my photos before the timer ends?

Nope! That’s the whole magic of the time capsule. Once it’s locked, not even you can view it until the countdown finishes.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All your uploads are stored securely with encryption. Only your account has access, and nothing is accessible until the set timer expires.

Can I delete a time capsule before the timer ends?

No, much like a time capsule, we believe that not being able to delete it mirrors the authentic experience of storing a time capsule.

What file types are supported?

Currently, we support standard image formats: PNG, JPG, and JPEG. More formats may be added in the future.

Is there a limit to how many photos I can store?

Actually, with the amount we are overseeing, we see that you can share as much as your heart's desire!

What if I forget my login or password?

No worries! You can reset your password via the login page. If you need help, our support team is just a click away.
