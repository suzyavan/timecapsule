About Us

Have you ever come across a news report about a time capsule that was buried 50 or even 100 years ago? While it may seem like those traditions belong to the past, they can still be brought to life through the Time Capsule. This platform was created to give users a secure and meaningful way to preserve memories—whether with family, friends, or during adventures—by uploading photos into a protected digital file. Users can select a specific date for their file to be unlocked, ensuring that the content remains inaccessible until that moment arrives. Our goal is to provide a trustworthy platform where users can store cherished memories, allowing future generations to experience and appreciate moments from the past.
